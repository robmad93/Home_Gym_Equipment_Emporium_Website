Background

About Rob

Rob began his career as a fitness instructor and later managed several commercial gyms across Ireland. After years of hearing clients struggle to find affordable, high-quality equipment for home use, he decided to create a business that put reliability, customer support, and accessibility at its core. The Emporium was born from that vision.

Founding of Our Company

Founded in 2019, Rob's Home Gym Equipment Emporium has successfully established itself as the leading purveyor of home gym equipment in Co. Clare. Our company's rise to prominence is accredited to our commitment to quality service. We only source our range of home gym equipment from the most reputable manufacturers, and we provide prompt installation of purchases at times that most suit our customers.

Mission Statement

Our mission is to make professional-grade fitness equipment accessible to every household in Co. Clare and further afield, ensuring safety, durability, and honest pricing without compromise.

Company Values

Our core values include:

  • Commitment to quality and safety
  • Transparent pricing
  • Customer-first service
  • Sustainable sourcing
  • Continuous innovation in home fitness solutions

Historical Timeline

  • 2019 - Emporium founded in Ennis
  • 2020 - Online store set up during the COVID-19 pandemic
  • 2021 - First warehouse expansion to include cardio equipment
  • 2022 - Launched installation service across Co. Clare
  • 2023 - Opened our second distribution centre in Kilrush
  • 2024 - Added smart home-gym technology to our catalogue
  • 2025 - Reached 2,000+ satisfied customers

Commitment to Quality and Safety

All products undergo multi-stage quality checks, including mechanical stress testing and safety certification. We partner only with internationally recognised manufacturers who comply with EU safety standards.

Sustainability Practices

We work to reduce waste by using recyclable packaging, partnering with eco-friendly suppliers, and offering a disposal service for old or damaged equipment.

Customer Impact

Since opening, we have helped thousands of customers create home workout spaces—from compact apartment gyms to fully outfitted garages. We regularly receive photos and feedback from clients who credit their home gym setups for helping them develop healthier, more active lifestyles.

Community Involvement

We proudly support local sports clubs and community fitness programmes, donating equipment and sponsoring events that promote health and well-being across Co. Clare and beyond.

Future Vision

As we continue to grow, our goal is to expand our product line, introduce more advanced smart-fitness solutions, and open additional service centres across Ireland.
